The two strategies

How each solver thinks

Strategy 1

Deep Explorer

This strategy commits fully to one direction, going as deep as it can before backing up and trying another path. Like someone who always follows a hallway to its end before turning around. Fast, but the path it finds might not be the shortest.

⚡
Often faster

Strategy 2

Careful Spreader

This strategy checks every nearby option before going further — spreading outward in waves from the start. Like someone who checks all doors on the first floor before going to the second. Guaranteed to find the shortest possible path.
